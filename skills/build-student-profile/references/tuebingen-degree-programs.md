# University of Tübingen Degree Programs

Reference list of the study programs offered by the Department of Computer
Science at the University of Tübingen. Use it to recognize the student's
`Degree program` in the profile, validate the thesis level, and ask the right
follow-up when the program implies a specific level or curriculum.

| Program | Levels offered | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Computer Science (Informatik) | Bachelor, Master | Broadest CS curriculum. |
| Media Informatics (Medieninformatik) | Bachelor, Master | HCI, visualization, media systems focus. |
| Medical Informatics (Medizininformatik) | Bachelor, Master | Clinical/health data and systems focus. |
| Bioinformatics | Bachelor, Master | Joint with biology/biochemistry; sequence and biological data methods. |
| Machine Learning | Master only | No bachelor track; assume a Master thesis. |
| Computer Science for Teachers (Lehramt Informatik) | Bachelor of Education, Master of Education | Teacher-education track; thesis expectations differ from the research Master. |

## Thesis duration

Official processing time once the thesis is registered:

- Master thesis: 6 months.
- Bachelor thesis: 4 months.

In practice the effective time is often a bit longer because students postpone
the formal registration. Use these durations to scope proposals: a Bachelor
thesis must fit a tighter 4-month scope than a Master thesis.

For the Bachelor and Master of Education (Lehramt) programs the thesis
requirements and duration can differ, so confirm them before scoping.

## How to use this

- Map free-text answers to one of these programs; accept German names too.
- Machine Learning implies a Master thesis. Do not propose a bachelor scope for it.
- The Bachelor/Master of Education programs are for future teachers; their thesis
  norms and scope can differ from the research-oriented programs, so confirm the
  expected format before generating proposals.
- The program is a hint about coursework breadth and domain, not a hard filter on
  topics. A Bioinformatics or Medical Informatics student can still pursue an ML
  thesis if their profile supports it.
